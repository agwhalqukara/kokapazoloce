Telkom Fibre Deals, Packages and Their Prices in 2023

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Telkom is stopping at nothing to improve its customer service and experience. With Telkom Fibre, users can now enjoy faster internet browsing speed at affordable rates. Another amazing thing about Telkom Fibre deals and packages is that the different packages come with different prices to suit different customer classes. If you are a heavy data user who uses a lot of data for work or even at home, you should absolutely consider choosing any Telkom Fibre packages.

If you don’t know them already, it’s nothing to worry about. This article will discuss the Telkom Fibre packages, how to install Telkom Fibre at home, and how to check if your location has Telkom WiFi coverage.

Telkom Fibre Packages And Their Prices

There is an ideal Telkom Fibre package for everyone. Telkom has two different Fibre packages – the home unlimited uncapped fibre and the Telkom business fibre package. These two packages come with different deals to accommodate the different types of customers.

Telkom Business Fibre Packages

This Telkom Fibre package is designed to benefit businesses like banks that need to stay online throughout working hours. They are different deals in this package to fit into the different budgets businesses may have. Telkom business fibre package include:

  • Uncapped Business 5Mbps DSL Premium Plan

Users of this package will enjoy unlimited data usage at R699 per month. This package comes with two IP calls and an ADSL speed of up-to 4Mbs. Users are also entitled to a free router, activation, and installation.

  • Uncapped Business 10Mbps DSL Premium Plan

This deal costs R899 per month, and its users will enjoy ADSL speed of up to 10Mbs. It comes with a free router, activation and installation, two IP call sessions, and Telkom Internet Mobile data.

  • Uncapped Business 20Mbps DSL Premium Plan

This business fibre deal costs R999, and the package comes with free router activation and installation, ADSL speed of 20Mbs, Telkom mobile data, and two IP call sessions.

  • Uncapped Business 40Mbps DSL Premium Plan

This Premium fibre plan costs R1,199, and users will enjoy an ADSL speed of 40Mbs. The deal comes with two IP call sessions and free Telkom Internet Mobile data.

Telkom Home Unlimited Uncapped Fibre Package

This fibre package has four different fibre deals – some of which are more expensive than others. The more expensive fibre deals are most suitable for customers who use more data and have a fatter data budget. Here are the different home unlimited uncapped fibre deals you can choose from:

1. 25Mbs Fibre Plan

This Telkom Fibre plan costs R699 and has a duration of one month. It is uncapped; therefore, there is no limit to how much data you can use for the month. Users of this fibre plan will enjoy the internet, both upload and download speed of 25Mbs. The plan comes with:

  • 2GB Telkom Internet mobile data
  • Free router and installation
  • 3GB storage email account

Users interested in having the voice call options can also request it, but it comes at an extra cost. The 25Mbs fibre plan is the cheapest and most affordable Telkom Fibre deal.

2. 50Mbs/25Mbs Fibre Plan

The 50Mbs/25Mbs Telkom fibre plan comes with two different payment options. Customers who choose this as their preferred fibre plan will enjoy more upload speed than those who use the 25Mbs fibre plan. In this plan, users will enjoy an upload speed of 50Mbs and a download speed of 25Mbs. This package has a duration of one month and will have to be renewed monthly.

This Telkom Fibre plan comes with:

  • 3GB Telkom Internet Mobile data
  • Month to month contract that includes free Router and installation
  • New users will enjoy free three months subscriptions
  • 3GB storage email account

Just like the 25Mbs plan, the voice calk is optional and comes with an additional cost. The price you pay for this plan will depend on the payment option you chose:

  • Home-Unlimited Openserve Fibre:  Users of this package will also enjoy unlimited data usage that costs R699, but from 6 pm to 11 pm, Telkom will regulate or control the data usage.
  • Home-Unlimited Premium Openserve Fibre: This deal costs R1,199 and has a duration of one month. Users get to enjoy unlimited data usage at all hours of the day.

3. 100 Mbps/50Mbs Fibre Plan

This plan is very similar to the 50Mbs/25Mbs plan as subscribers to this plan will enjoy an upload speed of 100Mbs and download speed of up to 25Mbs. The plan also has a duration of one month and will be renewed monthly. This plan comes with:

  • 3GB Telkom Mobile data
  • Month to month contract that includes free Router and installation
  • New users will enjoy free three months subscriptions
  • 3GB storage email account
  • Calling plan

It also has two different payment options, which include:

  • Home-Unlimited Openserve Fibre: This deal goes for R899, and users will enjoy unlimited data as well, but from 6 pm to 11 pm, data usage will be controlled.
  • Home-Unlimited Premium Openserve Fibre: This deal costs R1,399, and users can enjoy uninterrupted data usage at any time of the day.

4. 200Mbps/100Mbs Fibre Plan

This is the most expensive home fibre package, but also the deal with the fastest internet speed. Users who go for this fibre deal will enjoy 200Mbs upload speed and 100Mbs download speed. The plan also comes with 3GB Telkom Internet mobile data, an email account with 3GB storage free three months subscription for new users, and a free router, activation, and installation. Just like the other two fibre plans above, this plan also has two payment options which include:

  • Home-Unlimited Openserve Fibre: This payment option cost R1,169. Users will enjoy unlimited and uninterrupted data at all times except 6 pm to 11 pm, when the data usage will be regulated.
  • Home-Unlimited Premium Openserve Fibre: This deal costs R1,599, and users can enjoy uninterrupted data usage at any time of the day.  

How To Check If Your Location Has Telkom Network Coverage

For you to enjoy any of these Telkom Fibre packages above, your location must have Telkom network coverage. So, before paying for any Fibre package, you should find that out, and here is how to go about it.

  • Visit the Telkom Coverage map.
  • Input your location
  • Select the current location from the drop-down menu
  • Tap or click on the search icon

If you are fortunate to have Telkom network coverage in your location, you will be provided with information containing the network providers and packages.

For those whose location does not have Telkom network coverage, you will be requested to provide your details and tap the notify me button to be notified whenever that location has a Telkom network coverage.

Frequently Asked Questions About Telkom Fibre Packages

Here are answers to some of the questions you may have concerning Telkom Fibre Deals and Packages.

1. How can I set up Telkom Fibre at home?

  • Go through the quick reference guide.
  • Carefully follow the instruction on the Reference guide.
  • Connect the Modem to your cellphone and personal computer.
  • To set up your broadband modem, connect the Modem to WiFi and open a browser on the connected device. Then enter the URL on the belly label of the router. Follow the prompts on the screen.
  • Type in www.telkom.co.za/verifyadsl on your browser and input the required information to activate your broadband service.
  • Visit https://secure.telkomsa.net/titracker/ to set up your data usage notification.
  • You can now make use of the Telkom Fibre network by turning on your phone WiFi connection.

2. How fast is the Telkom Fibre?

Telkom Fibre can be as fast as 20Mbs to 40Mbs. It also has a Fibre package with a speed that is up to 100Mbs. The level of internet speed you experience will depend on the type of device you use to access the network and network coverage in your location.

3. How can I receive data usage notifications?

To check your Telkom data usage, all you need to do is login into the broadband usage tracker page using your username and password. Tap on the menu bar on the left and select notification, then select the notification frequency. Your notification will be sent to your registered email address.
