Tyga Claps Back At Soulja Boy After He Shades Blac Chyna And Says He Played Fortnite With King Cairo

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Soulja Boy and Blac Chyna have been recently involved for a few weeks, and then all hell broke loose.

If at first, rumors were claiming that Chyna and Soulja are together just to spite Tyga, then everyone believed that they were a couple when he posted a pic of the two of them on social media for Valentine’s day.

After that, Soulja decided to come clean with what’s going on regarding Chyna and why he dated her in the first place.

The Shade Room has the ‘shocking’ info and the message that he has reportedly posted beyond offensive.

After this whole thing, a message was tweeted with an apology from Soulja to Blac Chyna but later, he claimed that the woman hacked his phone.
