Jamin Ben “Jamie” Raskin (born December 13, 1962) is an American author and politician serving as the U.S. Representative for Maryland’s 8th congressional district since 2017. The district is located in Montgomery County, an affluent suburban county northwest of Washington, D.C., and extends through rural Frederick County to the Pennsylvania border. A Democrat, he was previously a member of the Maryland State Senate from 2007 to 2016.

Raskin is the author of We the Students: Supreme Court Cases for and about Students and Overruling Democracy: The Supreme Court versus the American People. Raskin co-authored Youth Justice in America with Maryam Ahranjani and Andrew G. Ferguson. In 1994, Raskin and John Bonifaz co-authored The Wealth Primary: Campaign Fundraising and the Constitution.
