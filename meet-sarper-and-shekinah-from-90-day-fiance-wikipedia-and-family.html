Meet Sarper And Shekinah – Wikipedia Bio

Sarper and Shekinah are a couple from the reality TV show 90 Day Fiance who are popular for their drama and conflict on-screen.

Their story is entertaining and captivating for the viewers who want to see if they will get married or not.
