What is Leighk.com?

This online store claims to sell many of the products listed above. Before you decide to shop at this online store, there are many things you need to know.

Leighk/ Amyashop has been classified as one of the suspect sites based on the following facts:

Different domain names and websites:

Its domain name i.e. Leighk.com is the website name. Amyashop is different from legitimate websites. It has used the same domain name instead of its domain name in its policy pages. This is mostly due to copy-paste errors.

Contact Information:

Its parent company, “Bin Estrella GmbH”, is listed on the “CONTACT US”, and “TERMS of SERVICE” pages. However, it has been found that multiple scam and troublesome sites like Sheoun and Vivianea, Qouwaguu and Shienmall, Wintertc, have used its name.

It has given Bin Estrella GmbH as its parent company, but it could change its address and name in the future due to similar sites.

Special Discounts and Sales Offers

Leighk lists many products for sale with huge discounts. These discounts are often used to lure people to scam websites.

Return and Exchange

Its Return Policy does not allow for any type of exchange or return of products. This online store does not allow for returns or exchanges under any circumstances. These online stores have confusing returns and/or exchange provisions that make it almost impossible to get your full payment back.

Our Final Verdict:

All the above-mentioned facts lead to the conclusion that Leighk/Amyashop is one suspicious website.
