Sara Bareilles Biography

Sara Bareilles is best known as Musician, singer-songwriter, actress who has an estimated Net Worth of $16 Million. Singer-songwriter whose single “Love Song” exploded in popularity and reached number four on the Billboard Hot 100. She won the ASCAP Pop Music Awards' Vanguard Award in 2008. She was born on December 7,1979, Eureka, CA. After one of her performances, the stage collapsed and about forty-five people were injured. She was 80th on the Top 100 Greatest Women in Music in 2012. Her singing career began with her participation in various choirs in high school and at UCLA. She played in local Los Angeles bars and cafés.  Michelle Obama asked her to perform at the G-20 Summit.
