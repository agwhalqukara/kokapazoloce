Angelina Jolie Cozies Up To Famed Director Amid Rumors She Is Ready For A Bombshell Interview About

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Angelina Jolie got extra cozy with director Alfonso Cuaron during the weekend screening of his new film, Roma. The actress is reportedly a huge fan of Cuaron’s work and looked more than happy accompanying him to the premiere of his latest movie.

Jolie rocked a black dress for the outing and posed for a few pics alongside the director. The event was held at the San Vicente Bungalows.

Jolie, of course, is still dealing with the aftermath of a nasty divorce from Brad Pitt and has not made many public appearances over the past two years, especially with a man at her side.

According to Radar Online, Jolie and Pitt are on the verge of signing a divorce agreement, which would put to end their longstanding custody battle. The two share six children: Maddox, Pax, Zahara, Shiloh, Knox and Vivienne.

The actress is also reportedly planning to release a shocking interview about her divorce and the ensuing custody war.

Jolie allegedly agreed to do the interview because she is sick and tired of how the split has ruined her reputation in Hollywood. She hopes the interview will set the record straight and help fans understand her side of the story.

That said, Jolie likely signed a clause that prohibits her from bad mouthing Pitt in public. That means her interview will probably avoid mentioning Pitt by name and attack him in a more subtle manner.

“These two hate each other still with a passion and she’s very keen to set things straight, even if it’s done subtly,” an insider dished.

Until the interview is released, it looks like Jolie is more than happy with how things are going in 2019.

It is unclear if Jolie is romantically involved with Cuaron, but it is only a matter of time before she finds love again.

As far as Brad Pitt is concerned, sources claim that he is worried about what Jolie might reveal in her bombshell interview. But unless she violates something in the divorce agreement, there isn’t anything he can do about it.
