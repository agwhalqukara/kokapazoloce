Chris Lowell Is First To Join Hilary Duff In 'How I Met Your Father'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 5, 2024

Chris Lowell has joined the cast of the upcoming How I Met Your Mother sequel series!

The 36-year-old actor is the first announced to join Hilary Duff in How I Met Your Father, which will premiere on Hulu.

Find out more about his character…

Chris will be taking on the role of Jesse, “who is smart, with a bit of an edge and cynical about love. He’s an aspiring musician who works as an Uber driver to make ends meet and lives with his best friend Tom,” according to Deadline.

How I Met Your Father brings us to 2021 where Sophie (Duff) and her close-knit group of friends are in the midst of figuring out who they are, what they want out of life, and how to fall in love in the age of dating apps and limitless options.

Chris recently appeared in the movie Promising Young Woman and on GLOW as Bash Howard. His other credits include Graves, Enlisted and Veronica Mars.
