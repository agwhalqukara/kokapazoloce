Alessandra Ambrosio in Bathing Suit Top Smiles With Pal Celebwell

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Model Alessandra Ambrosio met up with fellow Brazilian and PatBo founder, Patricia Bonaldi, and the two of them looked gorgeous in a new photo from Instagram. Bonaldi uploaded a photo to her Instagram story of the two of them wearing stylish tops, showing off a bit of their midriffs. (Makes sense. PatBO makes hand-embroidered ready-to-wear and swimwear collections.) How does the model stay so fit? Read on to see 5 ways Alessandra Ambrosio stays in shape and the photos that prove they work—and to get beach-ready yourself, don't miss these essential 30 Best-Ever Celebrity Bathing Suit Photos!

Ambrosio is a big fan of doing yoga, and has posted photos of herself doing certain poses on Instagram. In this photo, Ambrosio is seen in a bridge position with one foot in the air, while spending time at Malibu Beach. She captioned the photo, "Feeling grateful for Saturday mornings & salty air."

Ambrosio posts a lot of photos of herself going outside and getting fresh air. She is seen spending time at the beach, going on a hike, or even doing a photoshoot. No matter what activity she's doing, Ambrosio makes sure to get a lot of vitamin D and time in the sun. 

Ambrosio told The Edit that when it comes to her diet, she believes in moderation, and balance. She explains that she mostly eats healthy at home, with the occasional indulgence, and is less strict when she's on vacation.  "I'm all about moderation and eating something different every day. Obviously I try not to splurge on pasta, but if I'm back in Brazil where everything is pasta- or bread-based — and so yummy — I'll eat whatever I want, then go back to lots of salads and chicken or fish with veg as soon as I'm back in LA."

Ambrosio told FOX News that she does try to do some sort of physical activity each day, no matter how busy her schedule gets. She says that she likes to do planks and quick stretches when she has the time. Ambrosio says that she always stays motivated to work out and eat healthy, and she makes sure that her reasons for doing so are positive ones. "I think my motivation is more to stay healthy than look good," she said to Harper's Bazaar. Another of Ambrosio's motivators? Her two kids, as she thinks that being a mom has changed her outlook on wellness. "I think that my workout regime has changed more after giving birth to my children," she says.
