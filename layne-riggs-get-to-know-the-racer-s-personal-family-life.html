Layne Riggs: Get To Know The Racers Personal &amp; Family Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Layne Riggs began racing since the age of 10 with a strong influence from his father. He had a career breakthrough by winning the NASCAR Advance Auto Parts Weekly Series championship in September 2022.

Layne Riggs and his girlfriend has been dating since 2022. Know about his dating life, wiki, parents, family, college and professional career details here.

Layne Riggs is an American professional stock car race driver who competes part-time in the NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series. He drives the No. 1 Toyota Tundra for Tricon Garage, the No. 02 Chevrolet Silverado for Young’s Motorsports, and the No. 7 Silverado for Spire Motorsports.

Moreover, he competes part-time in the NASCAR Xfinity Series, driving the No. 11 Chevrolet Camaro for Kaulig Racing, as well as part-time in the CARS Late Model Stock Tour, driving the No. 99 Ford Mustang for his family-owned team, Riggs Racing, and the No. 62 Mustang for Kevin Harvick Incorporated.

Layne Riggs Parents, Family & Early Life

Riggs was born Layne Griffin Riggs on 11 June 2002 in Bahama, North Carolina, United States. He is the son of American parents, Scott Riggs and Jai Riggs.


The racer’s father Scott is also a professional stock car race driver who last competed in the No. 92 for RBR Enterprises in the NASCAR Camping World Truck Series. He competed in 208 Cup races from 2004 to 2013 and made 115 Xfinity and 60 NASCAR truck starts.

Layne’s father inspired him to pursue a career in racing. His father worked as the truck driver, the spotter, the crew chief, the car chief, and everything else on his racing team. As of now, his father runs a family-owned racing team named Riggs Racing.


An eldest of two children, he grew up with a younger sister named Skyler Riggs. He attended Voyager Academy High School and is now pursuing a degree in mechanical engineering at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.

Layne Riggs Girlfriend: Who Is He Dating Now?

Riggs is currently dating a beautiful girlfriend named Kristen Piercy. He has shared some pictures with his girlfriend on Instagram which has amassed more than 5k followers.

The couple has been together for nearly two years. They began their relationship after meeting in college in February 2022.


Kristen is attending the University of North Carolina where she is pursuing her bachelor’s in Communication Studies concentrating on Health Communication. She is a supportive girlfriend and is seen attending racing events with her boyfriend.

Won The NASCAR Advance Auto Parts Weekly Series Championship In 2022

Riggs started driving at the age of 10, racing in Limited Sportsman events at Orange County Speedway. He made his debut in the CARS Late Model Stock Tour on his 14th birthday on 11 June 2016.

Moreover, he won the Rodney Cook Classic at Ace Speedway in 2019. He won the NASCAR Advance Auto Parts Weekly Series championship in September 2022 by winning 16 races, 29 top fives, and 35 top tens racing.


Riggs became the youngest driver to win the championship, by beating Peyton Sellers who won it in 2005. He made his Truck Series debut in August 2023 at the Lucas Oil Indianapolis Raceway Park, driving the No. 7 truck for Spire Motorsports.

Likewise, he made his NASCAR Xfinity Series debut for Kaulig Racing in the Texas fall race in September 2023. He earned his first career top 10 after finishing 10th at Las Vegas.
