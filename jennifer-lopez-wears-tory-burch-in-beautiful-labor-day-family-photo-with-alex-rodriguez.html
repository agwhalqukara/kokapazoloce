Jennifer Lopez Wears Tory Burch In Beautiful Labor Day Family Photo With Alex Rodriguez

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 22, 2024

It’s hard to believe that Jennifer Lopez is 51-years-old. She’s one of the most beautiful women in Hollywood and has no concept of aging or slowing down. Her career is still at an all-time high and she has millions of fans who adore her and follow her every move. She sings, dances, acts, is an entrepreneur, and is the very definition of a multi-hyphenate. And she is no stranger to making fashion headlines, as she recently did over Labor Day weekend.

Jennifer spent Labor Day with her fiance Alex Rodriguez and their blended family including her twins Max and Emme that she shares with Marc Antony. Alex has two daughters that he shares with ex-wife Cynthia Scurtis — Natasha and Ella.

Jennifer posted a beautiful family photo on her official Instagram account, where she has 135 million followers. Alex followed and shared another photo from the day on his official Instagram.

It didn’t take long before fans shared the photos and wanted to know who made the gorgeous dress that Jennifer Lopez wore. The dress comes from Tory Burch and demand for the dress was so great that Tory also shared the photos Jennifer and Alex posted and explained that the dress is part of the SS20 collection.

Tory Burch stated the following along with several photos that she shared with her 2.4 million followers.

“Many of you asked about the dress Jennifer Lopez is wearing – it’s our Handkerchief Embroidered Gown from the SS20 collection, available now on our website #ToryBurchSS20 #ToryBurch @JLo #JLo.”

You may see the photos that Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez originally posted on their Instagram accounts below.

https://www.instagram.com/p/CE6v4kuArJe/

The children all sat in the tree while Jennifer and Alex posed at the tree’s base. Jennifer wore two hairstyles throughout the shoot. In the first photo, she wore her hair slicked back and loose with full curls in the second. Fans couldn’t help but say how gorgeous Jennifer Lopez looked and how beautiful her Tory Burch Handkerchief Embroidered Gown. The dress costs approximately $2,498.
